DECONSTRUCTED SANDWICH

ATTENTIONAL PRACTICE

Separate ingredients, eat in sequence, and log what you feel at each step. There is no “submit” server — your draft is saved in this browser as you type. When you are done, use Export my session to save a .txt file to your Downloads folder, or grab the emotional plate as a .png.

SETUP

Each ingredient is arranged individually on a plate — bread, protein, dressing, cheese, and vegetables are discrete elements, not assembled.

Download

Blank ruleset — instructions only. Export my session — everything you typed, as a .txt file (same as the bar at the bottom of the screen).

Emotional plate

Output is a wide rectangle with a circle inside — like a color-wheel target: concentric rings (one per eating step, outside → in) and radial slices for motion and contrast. The center is reflection. Colors come from what you typed; segment ripples add an op-art shimmer — not a diagnosis.

    Session

    Steps — what do you feel?

    After each step, note texture, urge, guilt, comfort, surprise — whatever shows up. There is no wrong answer.
